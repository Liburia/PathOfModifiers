[h1] Path Of Modifiers [/h1] 

Path Of Modifiers replaces vanilla prefixes with a comprehensive modifier and crafting system!

[list]
    [*]Collect modifier fragments from killed enemies
    [*]Use fragments in the modifier forge to roll and craft your items
    [*]Have fun with over 100 various modifiers!
[/list]
